The Accordian Leader Card Game

View the Rules and Objectives

The objective of the game is to have 5 cards left
A standard deck of 52 cards are used that are all layed out

The play

Your last card is your leader card, it may be placed on top of the first, second, and third card on the left if any of them match the suit or the number.

WARNING! The Game is Hard!

The odds of winning the game is a 1/100 chance(I did not know this, sorry)!
